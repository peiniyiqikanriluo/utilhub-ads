引言 / Introduction

极简工具箱（以下简称"我们"）非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策旨在说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和共享您的个人信息，以及您如何管理这些信息。

Minimal Toolbox ("we", "us", or "our") takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy aims to explain how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information, and how you can manage this information.

我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

为提供更好的服务体验，我们可能会收集以下类型的信息：

To provide a better service experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • 使用数据：当您使用我们的工具时，我们会记录工具的使用次数，以改进用户体验。
  • Usage Data: When you use our tools, we record usage counts to improve user experience.
  • 偏好设置：您的主题偏好（如暗黑模式）、收藏的工具等设置信息。
  • Preference Settings: Your theme preferences (such as dark mode), favorite tools, and other settings information.
  • 技术信息：浏览器类型、IP地址、操作系统等用于技术优化的信息。
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system, and other information for technical optimization.

信息存储方式 / Information Storage Methods

我们主要通过浏览器本地存储（localStorage）保存您的使用数据和偏好设置。这些信息仅存储在您的设备上，不会传输到我们的服务器。

We primarily use browser local storage (localStorage) to save your usage data and preference settings. This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Ezoic 服务 / Ezoic Services

本网站使用 Ezoic Inc.（"Ezoic"）的服务，包括管理第三方兴趣导向广告。Ezoic 可能在本网站上使用多种技术，包括用于提供内容、展示广告以及向本网站访问者启用广告的工具，这些技术可能使用第一方和第三方 cookies。

This website uses the services of Ezoic Inc. ("Ezoic"), including to manage third-party interest-based advertising. Ezoic may employ a variety of technologies on this website, including tools to serve content, display advertisements and enable advertising to visitors of this website, which may utilize first and third-party cookies.

Cookie 是由 Web 服务器发送到您设备上的一个小型文本文件，它使网站能够记住您的浏览活动信息。第一方 cookie 由您访问的网站创建，而第三方 cookie 由您访问的域名以外的域设置。Ezoic 和我们的合作伙伴可能会放置第三方 cookie、标签、信标、像素和类似技术，以监控与广告的互动并优化广告定位。

A cookie is a small text file sent to your device by a web server that enables the website to remember information about your browsing activity. First-party cookies are created by the site you are visiting, while third-party cookies are set by domains other than the one you're visiting. Ezoic and our partners may place third-party cookies, tags, beacons, pixels, and similar technologies to monitor interactions with advertisements and optimize ad targeting.

请注意，禁用 cookies 可能会限制对网站上某些内容和功能的访问，拒绝 cookies 不会消除广告，但会导致非个性化广告。

Please note that disabling cookies may limit access to certain content and features on the website, and rejecting cookies does not eliminate advertisements but will result in non-personalized advertising.

在提供个性化广告时，可能会在 cookie 中收集、使用和存储以下信息：

The following information may be collected, used, and stored in a cookie when serving personalized ads:

  • IP 地址 / IP address
  • 操作系统类型和版本 / Operating system type and version
  • 设备类型 / Device type
  • 语言偏好 / Language preferences
  • Web 浏览器类型 / Web browser type
  • 电子邮件（以哈希或加密形式） / Email (in a hashed or encrypted form)

Ezoic 及其合作伙伴可能会将这些数据与独立收集的信息结合使用，以跨不同平台和网站提供定向广告。Ezoic 的合作伙伴还可能收集其他数据，例如唯一 ID、广告 ID、地理位置数据、使用数据、设备信息、流量数据、来源来源以及用户与网站或广告之间的互动，以创建受众群体细分，用于跨设备、浏览器和应用程序的定向广告。

Ezoic and its partners may use this data in combination with information that has been independently collected to deliver targeted advertisements across various platforms and websites. Ezoic's partners may also gather additional data, such as unique IDs, advertising IDs, geolocation data, usage data, device information, traffic data, referral sources, and interactions between users and websites or advertisements, to create audience segments for targeted advertising across different devices, browsers, and apps.

您可以在此查看 Ezoic 的隐私政策，或者有关 Ezoic 广告和其他合作伙伴的更多信息，请在此查看 Ezoic 的广告合作伙伴。

You can view Ezoic's privacy policy here, or for additional information about Ezoic's advertising and other partners, you can view Ezoic's advertising partners here.
